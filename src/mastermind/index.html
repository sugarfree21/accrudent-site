How it works

AccruDent's Finance Leader Network is a place where finance leaders in dental can collaborate on shared challenges and gain access to exclusive DSO and dental group specific material.

This group is for dental finance leaders with these or other similar phrases in their titles: CFO, Finance Director, VP of Finance, Treasurer, Controller, FP&A Manager, Accountant, Bookkeeper, & more. We also have an exclusive group for doctors who are interested in learning more.

The group is live right now and accepting new members! Click on the link below to come to visit our membership platform and learn more.
