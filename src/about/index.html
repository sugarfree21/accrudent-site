Our Story

Getting Started

DSOs and corporate dentistry are still a new and growing industry. While we've all been able to learn a lot over the last 30 years, there are still many problems we haven't quite solved. Between dealing with insurance providers, getting accurate financial reporting, or working with dental boards in multiple states, operating multiple dental locations can be challenging!

Ken and Dallin have a combined 20+ years of experience working in dental. Suffice to say that any time this father-son combo got together, the dinner table conversation quickly turned to what challenges each was facing and how they could solve them. Eventually they decided that they needed to take their hard-earned knowledge and offer it to the world, and in Summer of 2024 AccruDent was born!

What's in a Name?

We get this question often: "where does the name AccruDent come from?" We definitely didn't start with it! After bouncing around from name to name, dealing with writers block and copyright issues, Ken and Dallin took driver's seat and shotgun on a ten hour car trip and threw out every idea they could possibly think of. The end result? Many protein bar wrappers, bleary eyes, and the name "AccruDent".

"Accrue" comes from the old French word "acreue", which means to grow, increase, or gather. The French language has special significance to Ken, who learned it while living in Canada as a young adult. The meaning of the word perfectly captures the mission of AccruDent, which is to gather the best minds in dental to help stabilize and grow DSOs.

Team

Our Team

Ken Kaufman, Co Founder

Ken thrives as an executive in high-growth organizations; since 2004, Ken has served in CFO and President/CFO roles in venture capital and private equity-backed organizations, helping to drive growth of 3 to 10x during his tenure at each. Ken founded AccruDent to help Dental Finance Leaders and DSOs grow and scale. Ken earned a business degree from BYU and an MBA from the University of Georgia.

Ken and his wife have been married for 26 years, and they have eight amazing children ages 26 to 9. Ken credits his wife, family, and faith as the source of any good he is able to accomplish!

Dallin Kaufman, Co Founder

Nothing is more fun for Dallin than leveraging modern technology in combination with good processes and procedures to solve business problems. He has been working around DSOs since he was 17, and is passionate about seeing them succeed.

Dallin is currently trying to balance his senior year at BYU while also pouring his time into AccruDent. He considers himself a pretty optimistic and energetic person. When not frantically typing on a computer or running between classes, you can probably find him hiking, running, or doing "nerd stuff" with his classmates.
