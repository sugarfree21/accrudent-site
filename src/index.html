Team

Our Team

Ken Kaufman (pictured left), Co-founder

Ken has nearly two decades of experience consulting and leading DSOs and group practices. He is a sought-after speaker and thought leader in the dental industry; make sure to check out his book, Financial Secrets to Grow Dental Organizations!

Ken earned a business degree from BYU and an MBA from the University of Georgia in finance & entrepreneurship where his peers selected him as the most outstanding student. He and his wife have been married for 25 years and have eight children. Ken enjoys serving in his church and uses whatever free time he can find to engage in cycling, mountain biking, and an occasional triathlon to keep himself humble.

Dallin Kaufman (pictured right), Co-founder

Dallin has spent several years focused on learning the technology that makes DSOs and group practices run. From simple computers and phone systems to complex networks and data harvesting, he understands how to leverage modern tools to elevate your practice to the next level.

Dallin is currently working on a cybersecurity degree from BYU while he isn't working on AccruDent. He enjoys hiking the mountains in Utah, running, and reading copious amounts of books later into the night than he should.
