About

Who we are

AccruDent was created for one simple reason: the DSO and dental group industry is complicated and growing, and new enough that there aren't a lot of answers to the questions we're all asking.

There are many consulting agencies out there that have chosen to focus on a specific dental office function: such as accounting, HR, or marketing. We've found that such an approach can be helpful, but ultimately isn't enough. Your accounting problems can't be solved without looking at RCM, your marketing problems can't be solved without looking at technology, and so on.

AccruDent seeks to do exactly this. We're ready to bring the resources of a full DSO to bear for your group of practices.
